Lesson 1: Basic Grammar

In this lesson, you will learn the fundamentals of English grammar.

Nouns

A noun is the name of a person, place, or thing.

  • Ali is a student.
  • Karachi is a city.
  • The book is on the table.

Verbs

A verb shows action or state.

  • She runs fast.
  • They are happy.

Practice

Write 5 sentences using nouns and verbs.
